My experience in Singapore Polytechnic

SP's Facilities

  Have you ever wondered what makes SP-ecial? My experience in Singapore Polytechnic has been a very pleasant one. In SP, there are plenty of state of the art facilities and amenities such as the SP sports arena, libraries and multi-purpose rooms.

  The study areas around each school block also provide a comfortable yet conducive environment to study. There are also a variety of food to choose from the different food courts as well, which provides flexibility.

Learning Environment

  Apart from this, the classrooms that I had my classes in were very well-maintained and they were of top-notch condition. Some aspects of what I enjoyed was the cleanliness and the school furniture.

  I also enjoyed the air-conditioned environment which boosts my productivity. I personally feel these are the key factors to make a study environment perfect for me. All this definitely helped me to assimilate well into my new and foreign SP environment.

My Experience in School Of Computing (SOC)

Modules (Subjects)

Other than comprehensive modules taught in the curriculum, there are many competent lecturers that conduct insightful and detailed lessons that never fail to excite me.

There are also Education career guidance (ECG) lessons as part of the curriculum which gives me a clearer view of my future paths as well as an overview of the current job market. It also helps me discover the jobs that suit my personality. Hence, I feel my experience in SOC would be an enriching and meaningful one.
